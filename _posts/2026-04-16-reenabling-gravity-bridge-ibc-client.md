# Proposal 5: Reenabling the Gravity Bridge IBC-Client

> NeutaroGuardian Voted: [YES](https://explorer.neutaro.io/Neutaro/tx/40D6F69B743EB7EB3C78F0113A13FB41C132F9945BE47ADA6FDB2A92909CE2BD)
{: .prompt-tip }


### Description
The Inter-blockchain Communication (IBC) Client responsible for the Neutaro<>Gravity Bridge for Neutaro-Osmosis is expired. The Timpi Development Team requests we re-enable.

#### Arguments In-Favor
The client used for this communication has expired, and we need to refresh it to reestablish the Neutaro-Osmo connection.

Osmo is a key partner chain. NTMPI tokens were transferred to Osmo earlier, and now that the client has expired, they are blocked. Refreshing the client will unblock them. This is also why we cannot simply install a new client, as the previously transferred tokens would be lost.

#### Arguments Against
I did not hear argument against prior to my vote.

### My Thoughts
Similar to my comments in [proposal #4]({% link _posts/2024-08-28-re-enable-neutaro-osmo-client.md %}) this is one of the OG partner bridges for Timpi and Neutaro. It's critical for the network to be able to increase access and ubiquity to the token and it's current holders. Because we already have NTMPI tokens distributed there, we should re-enable this connection to maintain that relationship cross-chains and for the best interests of Neutaro.

Considering these points, I believe it is fair and reasonable approve this proposal and re-enable the IBC client without undue risk to the chain.
